About

Small batches. Big intention.

Chilordinv began in Lagos with a simple idea — that wellness should feel like a ritual, not a checklist. We hand-pick every product that carries our name, from cold-pressed coconut oil sourced from family co-operatives to small-batch attars blended by perfumers we know personally.

Our shelves carry organic essentials, soulful perfumes, premium CBD, daily wellness supplements, and Longrich products you can trust. If it's on our site, it's been tried by us first.

Thank you for being here. Welcome to the family.
